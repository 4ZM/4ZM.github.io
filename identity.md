# Self

![SecretDouble](identity.jpeg)  

\-

How can it be,  
that I am me?  
Tomorrow and thereafter  

Nails grow and hairs diminish  
But I am he  
Tomorrow and thereafter  

Cell by cell replaced  
yet I remain  
Tomorrow and thereafter  

Will I persist?  
beyond the end     
Tomorrow and thereafter?
